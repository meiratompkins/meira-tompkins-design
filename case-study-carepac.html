Project Overview

Project Overview

CarePac, a leading packaging manufacturer, needed a comprehensive website redesign to transform their product pages from text-heavy, information-dense layouts into visually compelling, user-friendly experiences. The challenge was redesigning key product pages like Hang Hole Bags, Pouch Tear Notch, and Side Gusset Pouches to better showcase their products and improve user engagement.

Design Approach

I redesigned these critical product pages to leverage high-quality images, product mockups, and visual storytelling that transforms how customers understand and interact with CarePac's packaging solutions. The new design approach focuses on creating cohesive, flowing experiences that invite users to explore products while clearly communicating their benefits and applications.
