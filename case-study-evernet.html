Project Overview

EVERNET Consulting, a comprehensive IT and marketing services company, needed a professional WordPress website to showcase their diverse service offerings including IT support, cybersecurity, marketing services, and website design. The challenge was creating a modern, trustworthy online presence that clearly communicates their expertise and provides transparent pricing information to potential clients.

Design Approach

I designed a clean, professional WordPress website with a focus on user experience and conversion optimization. The homepage features a modern layout that immediately communicates EVERNET's comprehensive service offerings, while the dedicated pricing page provides clear, transparent information about their IT support, cybersecurity, and marketing services. The design emphasizes trust, professionalism, and ease of navigation to help potential clients understand the value proposition.
