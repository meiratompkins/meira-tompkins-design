Project Overview

Project Overview

The Tulane Energy Law Center needed a comprehensive Drupal website to showcase their academic research, faculty expertise, and educational resources. The challenge was creating a professional, user-friendly platform that could effectively organize complex academic content while maintaining the prestigious Tulane University brand identity.

Design Approach

I designed a custom Drupal solution with a focus on academic content organization and user experience. The design emphasizes clean typography, intuitive navigation, and professional imagery that reflects the institution's expertise in energy law and policy. The site features templated pages for leadership profiles, archive systems for blogs and resources, and a comprehensive content management system for ongoing maintenance.
