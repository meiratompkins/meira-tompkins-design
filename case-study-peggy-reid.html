Project Overview

Project Overview

The Peggy Reid Ogden Artwork website is an exceptionally crafted, cozy, and whimsical website. The site features the artwork predominantly but also highlights Peggy's life and her deep connection with her art. Incorporating flat artwork like sketches + 3D objects related to Peggy's life (beach shells, sand, and rock paintings + sculptures) helps to strengthen the connection between Peggy and her artwork.

Design Approach

The use of polaroid images also allows the user to experience the website as one would read a story and pushes the narrative aspect forward. This creates a unique, personal connection between visitors and Peggy's artistic journey.

10+ Custom pages built
WordPress Platform & CMS

Design Solution

Gallery Experience

The website features a clean, masonry-style gallery layout that adapts to different artwork dimensions while maintaining visual balance. The design prioritizes artwork presentation with minimal distractions, creating an immersive viewing experience that mirrors visiting a physical gallery.

Responsive Design

The website adapts beautifully across all devices while maintaining the artistic integrity and gallery experience. The responsive design ensures that artwork is properly displayed on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices, providing an optimal viewing experience for collectors and art enthusiasts.
