Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Website projects typically take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. Logo and branding projects usually take 2-3 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients outside your local area?

Absolutely! I work with clients remotely and have experience collaborating with teams across different time zones. Most of our communication happens via video calls, email, and project management tools.

What's included in ongoing support?

All website projects include 30 days of post-launch support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. After that, I offer maintenance packages or hourly support for updates and improvements.

Can you work within my existing brand guidelines?

Yes! I'm experienced working with established brand guidelines and can adapt my designs to fit your existing visual identity while ensuring consistency across all touchpoints.

What if I need a custom solution not listed here?

I love custom projects! These packages are starting points, but I regularly create tailored solutions. Let's discuss your specific needs and I'll provide a custom proposal.

What's your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% deposit to start, then the remaining 50% upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange a payment schedule that works for both of us.
