Project Overview

The Challenge

Growth Ladder Group, a business consulting firm, needed a professional website that could compete with larger agencies while working within a startup budget. They required a platform that established instant credibility, clearly communicated their value proposition, and generated qualified leads.

Why Wix?

The client chose Wix for its cost-effectiveness and ease of future content management. My challenge was creating a custom, professional design that didn't look like a typical Wix template while leveraging the platform's strengths.

My Approach

I treated this as a full UX project despite the platform constraints, conducting user research, competitor analysis, and creating a conversion-focused design strategy that maximized Wix's capabilities.

Competitive Analysis: Consulting Websites

Analyzed 25 business consulting websites ranging from solo consultants to major firms to identify best practices and differentiation opportunities.

Common Weaknesses:
  • Vague value propositions and consulting jargon
  • Poor mobile experience and slow loading
  • Lack of clear next steps for prospects
  • Generic stock imagery without personality
  • Complex navigation and information overload
Success Patterns:
  • Clear, benefit-focused headlines
  • Prominent case studies and testimonials
  • Simple, guided user journeys
  • Professional photography and branding
  • Multiple contact options and clear CTAs

Design Strategy & Wix Platform Optimization

Working Within Wix Constraints

While Wix offers great flexibility, creating a truly custom, professional look required strategic workarounds and creative problem-solving.

Challenge: Template Limitations

Problem: Wix templates often look generic and limit customization options

Solution: Started with a minimal template and built custom sections using Wix Studio's advanced design tools and custom CSS

✨ Achieved unique, branded look while maintaining Wix's user-friendly backend

Challenge: Performance Optimization

Problem: Wix sites can be slower than custom builds

Solution: Optimized images, minimized third-party apps, and used Wix's built-in performance tools strategically

⚡ Achieved 3.2s average load time, competitive with custom websites

Challenge: Mobile Responsiveness

Problem: Wix's mobile editor can be restrictive

Solution: Designed mobile-first, then adapted to desktop, ensuring optimal experience across all devices

📱 98% mobile usability score on Google PageSpeed Insights

Platform-Specific UX

Wix Can Compete with Custom Builds

With proper planning and execution, Wix websites can achieve professional results that rival custom development, especially for business consulting sites.

Mobile-First is Essential

Wix's mobile editor requires careful attention, but mobile-first design thinking leads to better overall user experiences across all devices.

Performance Monitoring is Critical

Regular performance audits and optimization are necessary to maintain competitive loading speeds on the Wix platform.

Business Consulting Websites

Credibility Drives Everything

For consulting websites, establishing immediate credibility is more important than flashy design elements or complex functionality.

Business owners need to understand exactly how consultants solve their problems - vague messaging kills conversions.
