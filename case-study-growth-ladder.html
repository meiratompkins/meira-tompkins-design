Project Overview

Growth Ladder Group, a business consulting firm, needed a professional website that could compete with larger agencies while working within a startup budget. This was a freelance project where I was tasked to create a website with Wix. They had no real brand guide except for the logo, which I used the logo colors to incorporate into the website to create a modern and user-friendly site.

I created two main pages: the home page and the services page. This was more of a complex project with payments because it had to accommodate a payment plan system for their coaching programs.

Design Approach

I treated this as a full UX project despite the platform constraints, creating a custom, professional design that didn't look like a typical Wix template while leveraging the platform's strengths for cost-effectiveness and ease of future content management.
