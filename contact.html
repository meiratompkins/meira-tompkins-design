FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical website design and development project takes 4-8 weeks, while larger projects may take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide and have experience collaborating remotely. I use various tools to ensure smooth communication and project management throughout the design and development process.

What's included in your web design service?

My web design service typically includes UX research, wireframing, visual design, responsive development, content management system setup, basic SEO, and training. Each project is customized to your specific needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, I offer ongoing support and maintenance packages for websites I've built. This includes updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates as needed.
