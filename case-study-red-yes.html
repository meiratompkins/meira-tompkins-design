Project Overview

Project Overview

The Red Yes Studio Website is a bold, rich, and 'punchy' website designed for a theatre production studio. The website focuses on clean typography and abstract/ethereal background imagery that conveys the diversity of work across different theatre productions the studio has developed.

Design Approach

The layout is very asymmetric, allowing the user to flow down the page in a 'dreamlike' state. This unique approach reflects the creative and artistic nature of theatrical work while maintaining professional functionality and responsive design.

Research & Discovery

User Research

I conducted interviews with 12 potential clients to understand their decision-making process when hiring a creative agency. Key insights revealed that portfolios, process transparency, and social proof were the primary factors.

Portfolio Quality

85% of users made decisions based on portfolio presentation within the first 30 seconds

Process Clarity

Users wanted to understand the creative process before committing to a project

Trust Signals

Testimonials and case studies significantly influenced hiring decisions

Competitive Analysis

I analyzed 15 competing design agencies to identify industry standards and opportunities for differentiation. Most agencies failed to clearly communicate their unique value proposition.

Common Pain Points:
  • Unclear pricing and process information
  • Poor mobile experience
  • Generic, template-based designs
  • Lack of detailed case studies
Opportunities:
  • Transparent pricing tiers
  • Interactive portfolio presentation
  • Process visualization
  • Real-time project updates

Lessons Learned & Next Steps

Key Learnings

User Research is Critical

The insights from user interviews directly shaped design decisions that led to higher conversion rates. Never skip the research phase.

Mobile-First Approach Works

Starting with mobile constraints forced better design decisions and resulted in a cleaner, more focused desktop experience.

Process Transparency Builds Trust

Showing clients exactly what to expect at each project phase significantly reduced anxiety and improved satisfaction.

Future Enhancements

  • A/B testing different call-to-action placements
  • Adding client portal for project updates
  • Implementing chatbot for instant inquiries
  • Creating video testimonials section
  • Developing case study template library
